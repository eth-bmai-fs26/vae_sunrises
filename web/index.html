The Decoder

Somewhere inside a neural network lives a tiny world of sunrises. Every point in this two-dimensional space maps to a unique image — a gradient of color, light, and atmosphere.

The horizontal axis captures something like time of year: cool winter blues on the left, warm summer golds on the right.

The vertical axis captures time of day: deep pre-dawn darkness below, bright midday light above.

Click anywhere on the canvas to decode a sunrise from its latent coordinates.
